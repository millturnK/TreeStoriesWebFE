Why Tree Stories?

We want everyone to be able to share their tree stories, whether of individual trees or whole forests and arboretums and enjoy reading others' stories. This wil democratise the sharing of such information, which is currently isolated in individual governmental asset management systems.

Browser Support

Tree Stories is fully supported on the evergreen browsers (Chrome, Firefox & Edge). The product works well on Safari and there are no known issues. The product works on IE11, however, in some installations there will be a font issue. IE11 will download fonts correctly if you change the Custom level of the "protected Mode" (under the Security Tab of the Internet Options Menu). After you click on the "Custom level..." button you have to search for "font download" and change it to "enable".
